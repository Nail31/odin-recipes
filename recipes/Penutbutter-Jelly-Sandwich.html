Penutbutter Jelly Sandwich Recipe

Nothing more simple but ever so fulfilling

Simply spread them to your delight and enjoy

Can always try different additions but nothing beats the classic

Ingredients

  • White toast bread
  • Penutbutter spread(with chunks is best I find)
  • Rasberry spread(Try to get natural spreads)

Instructions

  1. Toast both peices of bread
  2. Spread an even layer of both spreads of different peices of bread
  3. Combine both peices and cut in triangle and enjoy
