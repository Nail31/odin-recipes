Lasagna Recipe

Marinated beef or lamb ragu kept in company with cheese, bechamel and pasta sheets

Beloved by many for a reason, I usually make enough for two batches

Use a combination of cheeses to get more flavor, and add red wine to the meat when cooking it.

Ingredients

  • 1 Kg of ground beef or lamb
  • 4 Onions
  • 6-7 Carrots
  • Bechamel sauce (Store bought or own made)
  • Cheeses (Mozarella, Brie, Gouda, Eidam)
  • Tomato passata and red pesto
  • Pasta sheets

Instructions

  1. Sautee and caramlize onions followed by carrots
  2. Add Ground meat and (red wine to taste, adds a lot of flavor)
  3. Add tomato passata and red pesto and set to low heat
  4. Cook slowly for next 1 hour and allow to rest for another hour (Better even overnight)
  5. Begin to layout layers on backing dish
  6. Start with layer of meat, pasta sheet, bechamel, cheese, and then the process starts over
  7. About 4-5 layers depeding on thickness, always leaving a cheese layer on the top
  8. Cook for 25 minutes covered in foil sheet at 175 degrees followed by 185 for another 20-25 minutes
  9. Once cheese layer is golden, remove and rest for a few minutes and serve with your favorite glass of wine
