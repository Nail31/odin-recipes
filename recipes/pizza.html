Pizza Recipe

Simple and fresh ingredients make this come to life

You know what to do with the leftovers

Use a combination of cheeses to get more flavor, or add some peices of brie

Ingredients

  • Ready made pizza dough
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 Paprika
  • Cheeses (Mozarella, Brie, Gouda, Eidam)
  • Tomato passata and red pesto
  • Pepperoni or other Salami

Instructions

  1. Sautee and caramlize onions followed by paprika (season accoridngly)
  2. Begin to open pizza dough and drizzle some olive oil (turn on oven - 200 degrees)
  3. Add tomato passata and red pesto and spread over the dough
  4. Add a layer of cheese
  5. Add onion and paprika to the dough and spread. Top with cheese and Pepperoni.
  6. Cook for 15-20 minutes
  7. Once cheese layer is golden, remove and rest for a few minutes and serve
